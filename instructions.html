WELCOME TO QUIZZY!

A fun quiz game that can be played anywhere blabla catchphrase panalo.


HOW TO PLAY

  1. Choose a topic.

    2. After clicking the "START GAME" button, you will be given some topics that you can choose for the quiz. We are working to add more topics in our game soon!

  2. Quiz Time!

    3. After choosing a topic for your quiz, you will be answering 7 questions that are related to your chosen topic.

  3. Be Quick

    4. This is not that "take your time" type of quiz that you always find anywhere. You only have 15 seconds to answer a question, or you'll regret not answering one. Not a sight that you want to see.

  4. It's Done

    5. After answering all the questions, you will be given your score and see how well you did. Don't worry, if you're not proud of it, feel free to "PLAY AGAIN"
